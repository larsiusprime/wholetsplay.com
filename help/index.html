How can I help?

If you want to help, you can:

  1. Get in touch: email me at lars dot doucet at gmail dot com, or look me up on twitter.
  2. Donate some money
  3. Volunteer your time: we need web devs and lawyers. I've got some of both, but the more the merrier.
  4. Developers -- add your name to the wiki, and make your permission status known.
  5. Journalists -- write an article about #WhoLetsPlay!
  6. Youtubers -- talk about #WhoLetsPlay on your show.
  7. Everyone -- get the word out about #WhoLetsPlay. Tweet, share, etc.
